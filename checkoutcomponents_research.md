## Component Details

### Upsell

The Upsell component is used to offer additional products to customers at checkout. There are several types of upsell components:

*   **Checkbox upsell:** Used for optional paid or free products like shipping protection, gift wrapping, or donations.
*   **Free shipping upsell:** Shown to customers who are close to achieving free shipping.
*   **Large upsell:** Used to give a product more emphasis, like a sale item or a related product for a bundle.
*   **Radio upsell:** Used to offer a choice of products, like shipping options or donations.
*   **Upsell list:** A versatile component that displays a list of products for customers to add to their cart.

#### Supported Features

**Checkbox Upsell:**

*   **Appearance:** `Message Overide`, `Optional customer message`, `Options title`, `Enable specific mobile layouts`, `Product image formatting`.
*   **Conditional rendering:** `Markets publishing`, `Display rules`.
*   **Content:** `Title (optional)`, `Subtitle (optional)`.
*   **Product Sourcing:** `Manual Products`.
*   **Other:** `Hidden products`.

**Free shipping upsell:**

*   **Appearance:** `Border`, `Button text`, `Button type`, `Mobie variant dropdown style`, `Options title`, `Product image formatting`, `Scroll indicator`, `Products per page`, `Product count`.
*   **Conditional rendering:** `Markets publishing`, `Display rules`.
*   **Content:** `Title (optional)`, `Subtitle (optional)`.
*   **Product Sourcing:** `Collections`, `Manual Products`, `Automatic products`, `Integrations`.
*   **Other:** `Hidden products`.

**Large upsell:**

*   **Appearance:** `Border`, `Button text`, `Button type`, `Mobie variant dropdown style`, `Options title`, `Product image formatting`.
*   **Conditional rendering:** `Markets publishing`, `Display rules`.
*   **Content:** `Title (optional)`, `Subtitle (optional)`.
*   **Product Sourcing:** `Manual Products`.
*   **Other:** `Hidden products`.

**Radio upsell:**

*   **Appearance:** `Border`, `Button text`, `Button type`.
*   **Conditional rendering:** `Markets publishing`, `Display rules`.
*   **Content:** `Title (optional)`, `Subtitle (optional)`.
*   **Product Sourcing:** `Manual Products`.
*   **Other:** `Hidden products`.

**Upsell list:**

*   **Appearance:** `Border`, `Button text`, `Button type`, `Mobie variant dropdown style`, `Options title`, `Product image formatting`, `Scroll indicator`, `Products per page`, `Product count`.
*   **Conditional rendering:** `Markets publishing`, `Display rules`.
*   **Content:** `Title (optional)`, `Subtitle (optional)`.
*   **Product Sourcing:** `Collections`, `Manual Products`, `Automatic products`, `Integrations`.
*   **Other:** `Hidden products`.

### Content

The Content component is used to display messages to customers at checkout.

*   **Simple message:** Used to display a message to your customers, or display grouped content.
*   **Banner:** Used to display an important message to your customers.

#### Supported Features

**Simple message:**

*   **Functionality:** `Prefix Icons`, `Content align`, `Font size`, `Font weight`.
*   Also supports Markdown and Conditional Rendering.

**Banner:**

*   **Appearance:** `Variant` (Information, Warning, Critical & Success), `Collapsible`.
*   Also supports Markdown and Conditional Rendering.
