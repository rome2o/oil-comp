üìñ SEO Metrics Glossary

Clicks

The number of times users clicked on your website link in search results. Direct measure of traffic from organic search. Higher clicks = more visitors.

Impressions

The number of times your website appeared in search results, regardless of whether users clicked. Measure of visibility and reach. Higher impressions = greater visibility.

Click-Through Rate (CTR)

Percentage of impressions that resulted in clicks (Clicks √∑ Impressions √ó 100). Indicates how compelling your search result snippets are. Industry average for non-branded terms: 2-4% for B2B.

Average Position

Average ranking position of your website in search results. Position 1 is the top result. Lower numbers are better. Position 1-3 captures ~75% of clicks.

Domain Rating (DR)

Ahrefs metric (0-100) measuring website authority based on backlink profile quality. Higher DR = more authority. B2B industrial sites typically have DR 40-60+. Current: 30.1.

Backlinks

Links from other websites pointing to your site. Quality backlinks signal authority to Google. Dofollow links pass SEO value; nofollow links don't. Current: 2,908 total, 34.7% dofollow.

Core Web Vitals

Google's page experience metrics: LCP (loading speed), INP (interactivity), CLS (visual stability). Directly impact rankings. Theme change improved these metrics.

Organic Traffic

Visitors who arrive via unpaid search results (not ads). Most valuable traffic source. Current: 10,353 monthly visitors from Ahrefs estimate.

Keywords

Search terms users type in Google that trigger your website to appear. Tracked: 14,293 keywords. Top 10 rankings: 2,742 keywords (19.2%).

Branded vs Non-Branded Keywords

Branded: Include company name (e.g., "hbno essential oils"). Current: 5.9% of traffic.
Non-Branded: Generic terms (e.g., "bulk essential oils"). Current: 94.1% of traffic.

Statistical Significance (p-value)

Probability that observed change is due to random chance. p < 0.05 means 95%+ confidence change is real, not luck. All major findings in this report have p < 0.05.

Schema Markup / Structured Data

Code that helps search engines understand content, enabling "rich results" (star ratings, FAQs, product info in search). Theme change improved schema implementation.
