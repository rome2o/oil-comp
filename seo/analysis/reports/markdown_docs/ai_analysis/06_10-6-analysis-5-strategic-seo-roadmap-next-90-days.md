To: Allen, SEO Manager

Subject: 90-Day SEO Action Plan for HBNO.com / HBNOBulk.com
Allen,
Excellent work on growing impressions and improving average positions. The data shows significant top-of-funnel growth since you took over. The recent theme change has actually resulted in positive top-line metric movement (clicks, impressions, CTR, position), which is a great sign of a technically solid foundation.
Our challenge now is to capitalize on this momentum. The data reveals a significant opportunity to improve our Click-Through Rate (CTR), which is currently lagging despite high impression volumes. This tells us we are visible, but not compelling enough to click.
This 90-day plan is designed to address that gap immediately, then systematically expand our reach into valuable B2B keywords, and finally, build the authority to dominate our niche.

MONTH 1 (Days 1-30): Stabilization & Quick Wins¬∂Goal: Solidify the new theme's technical foundation, diagnose any hidden issues, and achieve rapid traffic gains by improving the CTR of existing rankings.

Task
Description

P0
Comprehensive Post-Migration Technical Audit
Use Screaming Frog to perform a full crawl. Identify and fix any 404s, redirect chains, canonical issues, or indexation problems introduced by the new theme. Pay close attention to mobile usability, site speed (Core Web Vitals), and schema markup integrity.
- Reduce internal 404 errors to zero.- Core Web Vitals (LCP, INP, CLS) in the "Good" threshold for 90%+ of URLs in GSC.

P0
"Striking Distance" CTR Optimization Campaign
Identify non-branded keywords ranking in positions 4-20 with high impressions but below-average CTR (<1%). Rewrite their title tags and meta descriptions to be more compelling, include CTAs, and better match user intent. Start with your top 50 opportunities.
- Improve average CTR by 20% for the targeted set of 50 keywords.- Increase clicks from this keyword set by 15%.

P1
Analyze & Fix Keyword Cannibalization
Use a tool like Ahrefs or Semrush to identify pages competing for the same keywords. Consolidate content where necessary, or de-optimize one page in favor of the stronger one to send clearer signals to Google.
- Resolve 80% of identified high-priority cannibalization issues.- See a measurable rank increase for the primary target URL.

P1
Optimize Branded SERP Experience
Your top queries are branded. Ensure the Search Engine Results Page (SERP) for "hbno" and its variations is flawless. Check your Google Business Profile, encourage reviews, ensure sitelinks are optimal, and see if an Organization schema is implemented correctly.
- Maintain a >60% CTR for the top 5 branded queries.- GSC shows rich results (sitelinks, reviews) for 95% of branded searches.

P2
Establish SEO Performance Dashboard
Create a Looker Studio (or similar) dashboard that pulls data from GSC, GA4, and your rank tracking tool. This will be our single source of truth for tracking progress against our goals throughout this plan.
- A live, functional dashboard is created and shared with stakeholders, tracking KPIs for traffic, rankings, and conversions.

Optimize Top 10 B2B Product/Category Pages
Revise the on-page content for your top bulk/industrial product and category pages. Integrate the B2B keywords from your research, add technical specs, use cases for businesses, FAQs, and clear calls-to-action (e.g., "Request a Quote," "View Spec Sheet").

P1
Implement a Strategic Internal Linking Plan
Systematically add internal links from existing high-authority pages (like popular blog posts) to your newly optimized B2B pages and new pillar content. Use keyword-rich anchor text. This passes authority and helps Google discover the new content faster.

- Produce a report detailing the content strategies of 3 competitors, highlighting 10 specific content ideas for HBNO to pursue.

Qualified Leads: Increase organic-driven "Request a Quote" form submissions by 20%.
Domain Authority: Increase Ahrefs Domain Rating (DR) from 30 to 33.

Risk Mitigation¬∂
Risk: Technical changes from the audit negatively impact the site.
Mitigation: Implement all code changes on a staging server first. Test thoroughly before pushing to the live site. Maintain regular backups.

Risk: New B2B content fails to rank or attract an audience.
Mitigation: Base all content on thorough keyword and competitor research. Don't just publish; actively promote the content through outreach and internal linking as outlined in the plan.

Risk: A major Google Algorithm Update disrupts our progress.
Mitigation: This plan is built on SEO best practices (E-E-A-T, technical excellence, user-focused content), making it resilient. If an update hits, we will pause, analyze the impact, and adjust the strategy accordingly rather than making knee-jerk changes.
