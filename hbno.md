# HBNO (IL Health & Beauty Natural Oils Co., Inc.)

## Overview

HBNO, also known as IL Health & Beauty Natural Oils Co., Inc., is a company specializing in essential oils, carrier oils, and related natural products. The company was founded in 2013 by Josef Demangeat and is headquartered in Chico, California.

## Key Personnel

*   **Josef Demangeat**: Founder and CEO

## Brands and Websites

HBNO operates under two main brands, each with its own website catering to a specific customer base:

### HBNO (hbno.com)

*   **Target Audience**: Direct-to-consumer (B2C)
*   **Products**: Essential oils, carrier oils, floral waters, and fragrances.
*   **Focus**: Retail sales of handcrafted wellness products made from handpicked, genuine herbs. The site also features a blog with informational articles about the uses and benefits of their products.

### HBNObulk (hbnobulk.com)

*   **Target Audience**: Business-to-business (B2B)
*   **Products**: Wholesale essential oils and a wide variety of other natural ingredients for personal care, home care, food flavoring, industrial use, and pet care.
*   **Services**: In addition to bulk ingredients, they also provide services such as private labeling, custom formulations, and dropshipping.

## Summary

In essence, HBNO is the parent company, with `hbno.com` serving as the retail arm and `hbnobulk.com` as the wholesale and manufacturing division.
