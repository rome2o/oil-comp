## üìã Project Overview

This proposal presents a comprehensive Shopify Plus growth strategy focused on recovering site health, optimizing conversions, and establishing sustainable B2B revenue growth through a managed team approach.

### Current Situation

- **Revenue Baseline:** $10,345 annual (Shopify data, 12-month analysis)
- **Site Health:** Currently compromised (theme change impact, technical debt)
- **SEO Position:** 14,293 keywords tracked, average position 20.1
- **Key Challenge:** High traffic potential, low conversion efficiency
- **Recovery Timeline:** 6-12 months for full optimization

### Proposed Solution

- **Managed Growth Team:** Multi-disciplinary expertise (SEO, CRO, Analytics, PM)
- **Flexible Service Tiers:** Starter ($1,500/mo) | Growth ($2,900/mo) ‚≠ê | Plus ($4,400/mo)
- **Phased Approach:** 90-day sprints with quarterly milestones
- **B2B Focus:** Quality traffic, lead generation, long sales cycle optimization
- **Proactive Methodology:** Continuous optimization, not reactive firefighting
