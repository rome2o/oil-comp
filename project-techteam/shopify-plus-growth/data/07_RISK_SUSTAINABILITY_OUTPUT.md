## ðŸ›¡ï¸ Workload Protection Guardrails

### Hour Allocation System

| Tier    | Monthly Hours | Weekly Cap | Daily Cap | Carryover Policy | Utilization Alerts | Over-Hours Process |
|---------|---------------|------------|-----------|------------------|--------------------|--------------------|
| Starter | 20-25 (HARD CAP) | 5-6 | 2 | None (resets monthly) | 80% (20hr) = warning | Change Order Required |
| Growth  | 40-50 (HARD CAP) | 10-12 | 3-4 | None (resets monthly) | 80% (40hr) = warning | Change Order Required |
| Plus    | 60-80 (HARD CAP) | 15-20 | 4-5 | None (resets monthly) | 80% (60hr) = warning | Change Order Required |

**HARD CAP ENFORCEMENT:**
- At 80% utilization: Client receives warning about remaining capacity
- At 90% utilization: Management review triggered
- At 100% utilization: All new work stops until next month or change order approved
- No carryover: Unused hours do NOT roll over (prevents accumulation and unrealistic client expectations)

### Core Principles

**1. NO UNPAID OVERAGES**
- Team members always paid fairly for their time
- Any work beyond allocation = Change Order + payment
- Protects team morale and retention

**2. CLEAR BOUNDARIES**
- Scope document signed by both parties
- Monthly tracking prevents surprise issues
- Change management process is fair and transparent

**3. TEAM FIRST**
- Employee satisfaction leads to better client results
- Burned-out team = poor quality & higher turnover costs
- Investment in team health pays dividends

**4. DATA-DRIVEN MANAGEMENT**
- Weekly time tracking (not estimates)
- Monthly metrics dashboards
- Early warning system for issues

**5. CLIENT SUCCESS & PREDICTABILITY**
- Client knows exactly what they're getting
- Clear hour budget prevents surprises
- Transparent pricing builds trust

## ðŸ“‹ Change Order Process

### Triggers for Change Order
- âœ“ Any request outside defined deliverables
- âœ“ Scope creep beyond monthly hour allocation
- âœ“ Major platform changes requiring rework
- âœ“ Client-requested priority changes
- âœ“ New discovery findings requiring strategy pivot

### Change Order Steps
1. Account Manager documents the request
2. Technical team estimates hours needed
3. Change Order document created with:
   - Description of work
   - Hours required
   - Cost ($XXX/hour or fixed fee)
   - Timeline
   - Decision deadline
4. Sent to client for approval
5. Work begins only after written approval
6. Hours tracked separately from retainer allocation

### Change Order Rates
- **Standard Rate:** $150-200/hour (depending on specialty)
- **Rush Fee:** +20% for work needed within 1 week
- **Emergency Fee:** +50% for work needed same day
- **Project-based:** Quoted separately for large initiatives
