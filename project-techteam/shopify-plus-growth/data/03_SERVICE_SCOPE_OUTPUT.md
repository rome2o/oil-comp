## Scope Management

### Hard Hour Caps (Workload Protection)

**Why Hour Caps Matter:**
- Prevents team burnout and overwork
- Ensures sustainable service delivery
- Forces prioritization of highest-impact work
- Protects both client and agency

**Enforcement:**

**Starter Tier (20-25 hours):**
- Weekly tracking: 5-6 hours/week maximum
- Monthly cap: 25 hours absolute ceiling
- At 20 hours: Warning to client about remaining capacity
- At 23 hours: New work paused until next month

**Growth Tier (40-50 hours):**
- Weekly tracking: 10-12 hours/week maximum
- Monthly cap: 50 hours absolute ceiling
- At 40 hours: Warning to client about remaining capacity
- At 45 hours: New work paused until next month

**Plus Tier (60-80 hours):**
- Weekly tracking: 15-20 hours/week maximum
- Monthly cap: 80 hours absolute ceiling
- At 60 hours: Warning to client about remaining capacity
- At 75 hours: New work paused until next month

### Change Order Process

1. Client requests additional work
2. Scope assessment (1-2 days)
3. Check hour capacity remaining for month
4. If over cap: Quote as change order or schedule for next month
5. Client approval required for change orders
6. Work scheduled & executed
7. Transparent tracking & billing

### Monthly Scope Reviews

- Review deliverables vs hours used
- Adjust priorities for upcoming month
- Discuss any scope creep concerns
- Realign on highest-impact opportunities
