- Even with 80% growth: $862 √ó 1.80 = $1,552/month

**This Indicates One of Three Scenarios:**

1. **Baseline Data is Incorrect** (Most Likely)
   - $862/month seems extremely low for an essential oils e-commerce business
   - Likely data extraction error or test environment data
   - Should be validated with actual Shopify revenue

2. **Pricing Needs Adjustment**
   - Growth Tier too expensive for this revenue level
   - Starter Tier ($1,500/month) still wouldn't break even
   - May need custom pricing or performance-based model

3. **Wrong Client Fit**

   - Better suited for DIY tools or fractional consulting
   - Needs to grow organically first before agency engagement

## Alternative Pricing Models

### Performance-Based Pricing

**Model 1: Base + Performance**
- Base Fee: $1,000/month (covers core work)
- Performance Bonus: 20% of incremental revenue
- Example: +$2,000 revenue ‚Üí $1,400 total fee

**Model 2: Pure Performance**
- No base fee
- 25-30% of incremental revenue
- Minimum fee: $500/month
- Cap: $5,000/month

**Model 3: Tiered Performance**
- 0-$2K incremental: 30% of increase
- $2K-$5K incremental: 25% of increase
- $5K+ incremental: 20% of increase

### Advantages
- Aligned incentives
- Lower risk for client
- Higher upside for agency if successful
- Validates confidence in ability to drive results

### Disadvantages
- Unpredictable revenue for agency
- Requires robust tracking and attribution
- Potential for disputes on results measurement
- May require longer contracts for stability
