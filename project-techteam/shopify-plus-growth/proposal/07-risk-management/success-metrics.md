**Trigger Conditions:**
- Metric at 90-99% of target
- Negative trend for 2 consecutive weeks
- Hour utilization <75% or >100%
- B2B inquiries <8 by Month 3

**Action:**
- Document reason for variance
- Adjust tactics if needed
- Discuss in next strategy call

### Red Alerts (Immediate Action Required)

**Trigger Conditions:**
- Metric at <90% of target by month-end
- Add-to-cart rate declining week-over-week
- Revenue impact <$100K by Month 3
- ROI <8x by Month 6

**Action:**
- Emergency strategy call within 48 hours
- Root cause analysis
- Pivot plan if needed
- Consider tier adjustment or program restructure
