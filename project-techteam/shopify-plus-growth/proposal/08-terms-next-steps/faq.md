### Q: Why the dual-track approach (Traffic Quality + Product Pages)?

**A:** Because your data shows two distinct problems:

2. **Product Page Conversion:** Even commercial visitors have a 2.03% add-to-cart rate (industry average is 4-5%). Your product pages are underperforming.

- SEO-only: Brings more traffic, but still converts at 2.03% (minimal revenue impact)

**Dual-track wins:** Better traffic (B2B/commercial) + better conversion (5.0% add-to-cart) = exponential lift.
