## üöÄ Critical Path Dependencies

### What Could Delay Timeline?

**Common Delays:**

1. **Access Provisioning (Week 0):**
   - IT approval process slow
   - Stakeholder unavailable to grant access
   - **Mitigation:** Prioritize access requests immediately upon signing

2. **Design Approvals (Week 2):**
   - Stakeholder out of office
   - Internal design review process
   - **Mitigation:** Designate backup approver, set 48hr SLA

3. **Payment Processing (Week -1):**
   - Check processing delays
   - Invoice routing issues
   - **Mitigation:** Use ACH or wire transfer for faster clearance

4. **Product Information (Week 1-2):**
   - Product details not readily available
   - Need to source images/copy
   - **Mitigation:** Gather assets during proposal review period

**How to Stay On Track:**
- Assign internal project coordinator
- Set calendar reminders for approval deadlines
- Over-communicate availability constraints upfront
