## New Insights Integrated from AI Analysis

### 1. Traffic Quality Crisis Identified

**Key Finding:** 90%+ of organic traffic has informational intent, not commercial

**Evidence:**
- Top keywords: "how to dilute tea tree oil for dogs" (1,400/mo), "benefits of helichrysum oil" (3,300/mo)
- Only <1% transactional traffic
- CTR collapsed 78% (from 5.47% â†’ 1.20%) due to intent mismatch
- 5.92% branded traffic indicates weak B2B brand awareness

**Impact:** Even perfect product pages can't convert visitors who came to learn "how to use tea tree oil on dogs"

### 2. B2C vs B2B Mismatch

**Current Audience:** Individual consumers seeking DIY wellness information

**Target Audience:** B2B bulk buyers (cosmetic formulators, manufacturers, spas, private label companies)

**The Gap:** Missing ALL high-value B2B commercial keywords:
- "bulk essential oil suppliers" (0 rank)
- "wholesale castor oil" (0 rank)
- "private label essential oil supplier" (0 rank)
- "essential oils for cosmetic formulation" (0 rank)

### 3. Mobile Bounce Rate Crisis

**Finding:** 85% mobile bounce rate (industry standard: 40-60%)

**What This Means:**
- 85 out of 100 mobile visitors leave immediately
- Critical UX issues on mobile devices
- With mobile-first indexing, this hurts SEO rankings
- Major revenue loss (mobile = 60%+ of e-commerce traffic)
