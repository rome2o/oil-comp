## Tier Upgrade/Downgrade Policy

### Flexibility

- **Review Quarterly:** Assess performance and needs every 3 months
- **Upgrade Anytime:** Move to higher tier with 30-day notice
- **Downgrade:** Move to lower tier at quarterly review
- **Pause:** Not recommended (loses momentum), but possible with 60-day notice

### Upgrade Triggers

Consider upgrading when:
- Current tier results exceed expectations
- Budget available for faster progress
- Competitive pressure requires acceleration
- Internal team bandwidth increases

### Downgrade Triggers

Consider downgrading when:
- Budget constraints
- Results plateau (though rare with continuous optimization)
- Internal team bandwidth decreases
