## Email Automation Sequences

### 1. Welcome Series (B2B Wholesale Buyers)
**5-7 email sequence over 14 days**

**Goal:** Educate new leads, build trust, guide to sales conversation

**Email Sequence:**
1. **Day 0:** Welcome + What to Expect (introduce HBNO, set expectations)
2. **Day 2:** Why Wholesale Buyers Choose HBNO (certifications, quality, pricing)
3. **Day 5:** Industry-Specific Use Cases (soap making, candle making, cosmetics)
4. **Day 7:** Bulk Pricing & MOQ Explained (transparency on pricing tiers)
5. **Day 10:** Private Label Capabilities (custom formulations, packaging)
6. **Day 14:** Ready to Order? (clear CTA, schedule consultation)

**Outcome:** Qualified leads ready for sales conversation

---

### 2. Abandoned Cart Recovery (Beyond Shopify's Basic)
**3 email sequence over 48 hours**

**Goal:** Recover lost revenue from cart abandoners

**Email Sequence:**
1. **+1 hour:** "Did you forget something?" (reminder with cart preview)
2. **+24 hours:** "Questions about your order?" (address common objections)
3. **+48 hours:** "Last chance" (urgency, limited-time incentive if appropriate)

**Outcome:** 15-25% cart recovery rate (industry standard)

---

### 3. Post-Purchase Nurture
**4-6 email sequence over 60 days**

**Goal:** Build loyalty, encourage repeat orders, gather feedback

**Email Sequence:**
1. **Day 1:** Order confirmation + What's Next
2. **Day 7:** How to Store & Use Your Oils (education)
3. **Day 14:** Need More? Reorder Link (convenience)
4. **Day 30:** Feedback Request (testimonial gathering)
5. **Day 45:** Explore Other Products (cross-sell)
6. **Day 60:** Exclusive Loyalty Offer (retention)

**Outcome:** Higher lifetime value, repeat purchase rate

### 4. B2B Lead Nurture (RFQ Form Submitters)

9. **Day 60:** Exclusive New Customer Discount (incentive)

**Outcome:** Higher RFQ-to-customer conversion rate

## Frequently Asked Questions

### "What if I don't have an email list yet?"

**Start building now:**
- RFQ forms capture leads (already in place)
- Product page opt-ins ("Get bulk pricing updates")
- Lead magnets (see [Content Marketing](./content-marketing.md))

**Month 1-3:** Focus on list growth (aim for 500-1,000 subscribers)  
**Month 3-6:** Start email automation (nurture growing list)

### "Can I manage email marketing in-house?"

**Yes**, but consider:
- **Time required:** 20-30 hours/month (strategy, writing, design, scheduling)
- **Expertise needed:** B2B email best practices, automation workflows, segmentation
- **Platform costs:** Klaviyo ~$150-300/month

**Hybrid model:** We provide strategy & setup, you execute campaigns (reduced fee: $2,000/month)

### "What's the difference between Shopify Email and Klaviyo?"

| Feature | Shopify Email | Klaviyo |
|---------|---------------|---------|
| **Cost** | $0.001/email | ~$150-300/month (5,000 contacts) |
| **Automation** | Basic | Advanced (behavior-triggered) |
| **Segmentation** | Limited | Deep segmentation |
| **Reporting** | Basic | Advanced analytics |
| **Best For** | Small lists, basic needs | B2B, growing lists, advanced needs |

**Recommendation:** Start with Shopify Email, migrate to Klaviyo at 2,000+ subscribers.

### "How do you handle unsubscribes?"

- **Unsubscribe link** in every email (legal requirement)
- **Preference center:** Let subscribers choose email frequency/topics
- **Re-engagement campaigns:** Win back inactive subscribers before removal

**Healthy unsubscribe rate:** 0.2-0.5% per email (cleaning disengaged subscribers improves deliverability)
