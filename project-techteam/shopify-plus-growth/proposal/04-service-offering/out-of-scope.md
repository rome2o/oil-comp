### 1. Paid Advertising Management

**NOT Included:**
- Google Ads (search, shopping, display)
- Facebook/Instagram Ads
- TikTok, Pinterest, or other paid social
- Retargeting/remarketing campaigns
- Paid influencer collaborations

**Why Excluded:**
