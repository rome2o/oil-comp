## üí∞ Retainer Model Structure

### Tier 1: Starter ($X,XXX/month)
- Monthly optimization review
- Up to 20 hours/month
- Specific deliverables (e.g., 2 conversion tests, 1 campaign)
- Reporting & analytics

### Tier 2: Growth ($X,XXX/month)
- Bi-weekly optimization reviews
- Up to 40 hours/month
- Expanded deliverables (e.g., 4 conversion tests, 2 campaigns, A/B testing)
- Advanced analytics & insights

### Tier 3: Plus ($X,XXX/month)
- Weekly optimization reviews
- Up to 60 hours/month
- Full-service deliverables
- Dedicated strategist
- Advanced analytics & AI-powered recommendations

**Each tier includes built-in workload caps to prevent overwork**
